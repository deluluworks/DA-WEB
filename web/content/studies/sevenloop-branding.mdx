## Case study

Sevenloop — branding and project brochure design. A comprehensive brand identity built to carry a precision-manufacturing platform into enterprise sales conversations, from the logo lockup through to a printed brochure designed to be left behind and remembered.

The Sevenloop brand mark is an orange circle with a four-leaf clover at its centre — a warm, ownable symbol for a company that sits between old-world manufacturing and AI-driven engineering.

## The work

- **Logo lockup** — the primary Sevenloop wordmark and mark.
- **Brand identity** — the logo system, colour, and type that give the platform a consistent, ownable texture.
- **Project brochure** — spreads built for the hand-off moment of an enterprise sales conversation.
- **Brand applications & collateral** — the identity extended across the touchpoints that close deals.
- **Brochure detail / mockups** — the printed system in context.
